Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated · July 2026

This policy explains how refunds and cancellations work for WSM Academy subscriptions, operated by WSM Investments Holdings (Pty) Ltd.

1. Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription for any skill track at any time — no lock-in contracts, no minimum term.

Important: Cancellation takes effect immediately. As soon as your cancellation is processed, your access to that track's AI tutor and tier-specific content ends right away — access does not continue until the end of your current paid billing period.

You will not be charged again after cancellation, but no partial refund is issued for the unused portion of the current billing cycle.

2. Refunds

Because your AI tutor and course content unlock immediately upon payment, WSM Academy operates on a limited refund policy:

Refunds are issued only when the technical fault is on our end — for example, if payment was successfully charged but your subscription tier failed to unlock, if you were charged in error (such as a duplicate charge), or if a platform malfunction prevented you from accessing content you paid for.

Refunds are not issued for:

  • Change of mind after enrolling
  • Not using the AI tutor or course content during a paid period
  • Dissatisfaction with course pacing, difficulty, or content style
  • Requests made after a subscription has already renewed for a new billing cycle

3. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you've experienced a technical issue on our end that qualifies for a refund:

  • Contact us within 7 days of the charge in question
  • Email wsminvestmentsholdings@gmail.com or WhatsApp 072 335 5470
  • Include the email address used to enroll, the skill track and plan affected, and a description of the issue

We aim to review and respond to refund requests within 5 business days. Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method via Paystack and may take 5–10 business days to reflect, depending on your bank.

4. Failed or Disputed Payments

If a renewal payment fails (e.g., expired card, insufficient funds), your access to that track's AI tutor is suspended until payment succeeds or you cancel. We do not charge additional penalty fees for failed payments.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
